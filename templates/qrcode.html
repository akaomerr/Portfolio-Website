QR Code Generator

QR (Quick Response) code, developed by Denso Wave, a subsidiary of Toyota, in 1994, is a two-dimensional barcode that can store a large amount of information. It was initially created for the automotive industry to track vehicles during manufacturing and logistics processes. However, its potential for broader applications quickly became evident.

QR codes gained popularity with the widespread use of smartphones equipped with cameras and barcode scanning apps. The codes can be easily scanned using a smartphone's camera, allowing users to quickly access information or perform specific actions. QR codes can store various types of data, including website URLs, contact information, text, and multimedia content.

The code I wrote for this project:

            
import qrcode
data=""#Type what the output of the QR code will be. 
qr=qrcode.QRCode(version=1,box_size=10,border=8) #Version:QR code version, box_size:size of the QR code, border:the size of the scanned portion.
error_correction=qrcode.constants.ERROR_CORRECT_L
qr.add_data(data)
qr.make(fit=True)
img = qr.make_image(fill_color="black", back_color="white")#You can choose the colors of the QR code.
img.save("QRcode.png")#Enter the name and type of the saved file.
