Audio Transmission Via Fiber Optic Cable

The first circuit is the audio signal receiver circuit. Used circuit elements; IR receiver led, 680K Resistor, 220nF-10pF-100nF-100uF(x2) Capacitor, LM386N, Speaker, +9V Power Supply.

The second circuit is the audio signal transmitter circuit. Used circuit elements; IR transmitter led, 100K-100 Resistor, 4.7uF Capacitor, BC547, JAK Cable, +9V Power Supply.
